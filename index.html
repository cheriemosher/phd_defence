Welcome to My Defence Presentation

Curious Case of the Coastal Tailed Frog (Ascaphus truei) Near the Northern Extent of Its Range

Thank you for attending this remote defence. These webpages show my work through text, tables, graphs, and images. Links to extra information are included as resources. Please explore! I hope you enjoy. Long live cloacal-extension frogs!

I would like to start by acknowledging that the land on which we would have gathered is the traditional unceded territory of the Lheidli T'enneh. Prince George and the surrounding area will always hold a special place in my heart and it was a great privilege to have lived there.



Portland, Oregon is my new home. I would like to acknowledge that the Portland Metro area rests on traditional village sites of the Multnomah, Wasco, Cowlitz, Kathlamet, Clackamas, Bands of Chinook, Tualatin, Kalapuya, Molalla. Included are resources for information and opportunities for support related to this region.



It was my honor to do research on Ascaphus truei, a frog who inhabits coastal mountains of northwestern North America. I would like to acknowledge the traditional territories of the Ts'msyen, Nisga'a, Haisla, Gitxsan, and Wet'suwet'en.
