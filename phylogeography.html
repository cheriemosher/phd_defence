Part 1

Tracking the northern range expansion using two types of genetic markers

I used microsatellite and genotype-by-sequencing to compare the within region genetic diversity of populations near the northern extent of the species’ range (British Columbia, Canada) to two geographic regions in British Columbia and two in Washington, USA, moving towards the core of the range.










Why northern range expansion?
